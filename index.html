Biography

Krishay Prakash is a student author from Bihar, India, who writes atmospheric mystery and fantasy stories inspired by folklore, memory, and time. His debut project, Swaraj Chaturvedi Adventures: The Cursed Hour, explores haunted places, ancient clocks, and the human courage needed to face the unknown.

Beyond writing, Krishay is interested in interactive storytelling and is adapting his fictional world into a 3D adventure game. His mission is to build stories that stay with readers long after the final page.

Sample Chapter: The Cursed Hour (Preview)

The bell of Vritanagar rang at midnight—once, twice, then thirteen times. Swaraj froze in the rain-soaked street. The tower clock above the ruined chapel had no hands, yet its shadow moved like a living creature across the stones. A whisper slid through the wind: "When the cursed hour returns, the town will remember what it buried." He turned toward the old castle hill. In the distance, a blue light burned behind a sealed iron gate that no one had opened for fifty years.
